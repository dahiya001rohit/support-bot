Unopened products: full refund within 15 days of delivery. Opened supplements cannot be refunded for safety reasons, BUT if you dislike the taste of your first order of any flavor, our Taste Guarantee applies: one free flavor swap per product, you only pay Rs. 99 shipping. Refunds are processed in 5-7 business days. Subscription orders follow the same policy per shipment.

## Product Usage Guidance
- Whey protein: 1 scoop (30g) with 200-250ml water or milk, post-workout or anytime to meet protein goals
- Creatine monohydrate: 3-5g daily, any time, consistently — loading phase optional
- Pre-workout: half scoop to assess tolerance first; do not exceed 1 scoop in 24 hours; avoid within 5 hours of sleep
- Always consult a physician if pregnant, nursing, under 18, or on medication

## Certifications & Quality
All products are FSSAI licensed, GMP manufactured, and tested by NABL-accredited labs for heavy metals, amino spiking, and label accuracy. Batch-wise certificates of analysis are published at fitfuel.in/lab-reports. We never use banned substances; products are safe for tested athletes (Informed Choice certification in progress).
